COOKIE POLICY

Our Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your experience while respecting your privacy and security.

Last updated: December 2025

1. Purpose of Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and prevent multiple exam attempts. Cookies help us provide better functionality on the frontend.

Security Cookies

Used to protect exam integrity and prevent unauthorized access or multiple attempts.

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that help maintain your session and preferences during your visit.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve functionality.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

2. No Third-Party Access

We do not share or allow any third party to access user cookies. We use cookies solely for user experience and exam integrity.

Strict Privacy Protection

All cookies used on AlaminEdge are first-party cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or share any cookie data with third-party services. Your browsing data stays private.

3. User Control

You can clear cookies anytime in your browser. Doing so may affect certain features but will not compromise your privacy.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
